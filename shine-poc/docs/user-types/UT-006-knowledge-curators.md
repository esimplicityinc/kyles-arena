# Knowledge Curators

## Overview

Knowledge Curators are the critical role for the Gold/Silver/Bronze content flywheel. They serve as domain stewards who validate submissions, promote high-quality content, and maintain the overall health of their knowledge domains. Without active curators, content quality degrades and trust in the system erodes. SHINE must make their curation work efficient and visible.

## Key Workflows

1. **Submission Review** - Evaluate new content against quality standards and domain relevance
2. **Content Promotion** - Advance validated content from bronze to silver to gold status
3. **Staleness Audit** - Identify and flag content that needs updating or retirement
4. **Template Maintenance** - Create and update submission templates and guidelines
5. **Domain Health Monitoring** - Track coverage, quality distribution, and engagement metrics

## Interaction Pattern

- **Entry Point:** Review queue or domain dashboard
- **Session Length:** 15-30 minutes (batch review sessions)
- **Primary Actions:** Approve, reject, request changes, promote, flag, archive
- **Preferred Output:** Review queues, domain health dashboards, staleness reports

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Review turnaround | < 48 hours | Keep content flowing |
| Gold content ratio | > 20% | Quality tier health |
| Content freshness | < 10% stale | Active maintenance |
| First-submission approval | > 50% | Clear submission standards |

> **Note:** This role is essential to SHINE's value proposition. If curators disengage, the content quality flywheel stops spinning.
