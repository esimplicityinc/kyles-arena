# New Joiners

## Overview

New Joiners represent a temporary but critical user state. Regardless of their eventual role, everyone spends their first 90 days in a mode of rapid learning and orientation. They need guided experiences that help them discover what exists without being overwhelmed. SHINE should recognize this state and provide tailored onboarding journeys that accelerate time-to-productivity.

## Key Workflows

1. **Guided Onboarding** - Follow structured learning paths appropriate to their role
2. **Discovery Search** - Ask broad questions like "How do I...?" or "Where can I find...?"
3. **Progress Tracking** - See what they've completed and what's recommended next
4. **Resource Bookmarking** - Save important resources for quick access later
5. **Mentor Connection** - Find people who can help answer questions

## Interaction Pattern

- **Entry Point:** Onboarding dashboard or guided path
- **Session Length:** 15-30 minutes (daily learning sessions)
- **Primary Actions:** Follow paths, search broadly, bookmark, track progress
- **Preferred Output:** Curated content, step-by-step guides, progress indicators

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Onboarding completion | > 80% | Engagement with guided content |
| Time to first contribution | < 60 days | Measure productivity ramp |
| Return visits (first 30 days) | > 15 | Building the habit |
| Self-service success | > 70% | Finding answers without escalation |

> **Note:** New Joiners transition to their primary role-based user type after 90 days, but the onboarding experience shapes their long-term relationship with SHINE.
