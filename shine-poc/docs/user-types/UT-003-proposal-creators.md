# Proposal Creators

## Overview

Proposal Creators are the content engine of the capture process. They transform requirements, past performances, and technical approaches into compelling proposal narratives. Working under tight deadlines, they need tools that accelerate drafting while maintaining quality. SHINE serves as both their research tool (finding examples) and their drafting assistant (generating initial content).

## Key Workflows

1. **Template Selection** - Find and apply appropriate templates for specific proposal sections
2. **Example Search** - Locate similar past proposals or sections to use as reference
3. **AI-Assisted Drafting** - Generate initial drafts based on prompts and source material
4. **Content Submission** - Submit drafted content for review in the approval workflow
5. **Revision Cycle** - Iterate on content based on reviewer feedback

## Interaction Pattern

- **Entry Point:** Specific section or template search, or AI drafting interface
- **Session Length:** 1-3 hours (extended creation sessions)
- **Primary Actions:** Search, generate, edit, submit for review, revise
- **Preferred Output:** Editable drafts, templates with placeholders, example snippets

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Draft generation time | 50% reduction | Measure productivity gain |
| Template reuse rate | > 70% | Consistency and efficiency |
| First-pass approval rate | > 60% | Quality of AI-assisted content |
| Content submissions/week | Baseline + 30% | Increased throughput |
