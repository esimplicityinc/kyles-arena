# Executive Leadership

## Overview

Executive Leadership users are senior decision-makers who need rapid access to organizational knowledge without diving into details. They consume information at a strategic level, asking questions like "What's our experience in healthcare AI?" or "Who are our top experts in federal compliance?" They rely on SHINE to surface synthesized insights that inform strategic decisions, client conversations, and resource allocation.

## Key Workflows

1. **Strategic Query** - Ask natural language questions about organizational capabilities and get executive-level summaries
2. **Portfolio Review** - View dashboards showing knowledge assets, expertise distribution, and capability gaps
3. **Trend Analysis** - Monitor what topics are gaining traction, what's being searched, what's being created
4. **Delegation** - Forward detailed queries to appropriate team members for deep-dive analysis

## Interaction Pattern

- **Entry Point:** Dashboard or quick search from homepage
- **Session Length:** 2-5 minutes (brief, focused)
- **Primary Actions:** Search, view summaries, export for presentations, share with team
- **Preferred Output:** Executive summaries, visualizations, bullet points, trends

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Time to answer | < 30 seconds | Executives have limited time |
| Query satisfaction | > 85% | Answers must be actionable |
| Return frequency | Weekly | Habitual use indicates value |
| Dashboard engagement | > 3 min/session | Meaningful consumption of insights |
