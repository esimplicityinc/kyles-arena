# Growth & Capture

## Overview

Growth & Capture users are the front line of business development. During active pursuits, they work under intense time pressure to assemble compelling narratives backed by proof points. They need to quickly find past performances, relevant case studies, and expertise that matches opportunity requirements. SHINE is their primary tool for answering "Have we done this before?" and "Who knows about this?"

## Key Workflows

1. **Past Performance Search** - Find relevant contracts, projects, and outcomes by customer, domain, or capability
2. **Proof Point Assembly** - Gather metrics, testimonials, and success stories for specific pursuit themes
3. **Expert Identification** - Locate SMEs who can contribute to proposals or client conversations
4. **Competitive Intelligence** - Understand what differentiates our approach in specific markets

## Interaction Pattern

- **Entry Point:** Targeted search with specific criteria (customer, technology, domain)
- **Session Length:** 15-45 minutes (deep dive during pursuits)
- **Primary Actions:** Search, filter, bookmark, export, share with pursuit team
- **Preferred Output:** Structured data (past perf details), exportable snippets, contact info for SMEs

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Past perf found | < 5 minutes | Speed is critical in pursuits |
| Relevant results in top 5 | > 80% | Reduce time sifting through noise |
| Bookmarked items reused | > 50% | Building a personal toolkit |
| Pursuit team sharing | > 3x per pursuit | Collaboration indicator |
