# Product & Strategy

## Overview

Product & Strategy users operate at the intersection of execution and planning. They need visibility across programs and projects to identify patterns, gaps, and opportunities. SHINE provides them with analytics and cross-cutting views that inform decisions about where to invest, what to standardize, and how to measure success. They're the connective tissue that helps the organization learn from itself.

## Key Workflows

1. **Portfolio Analysis** - Review knowledge assets across domains to identify gaps and overlaps
2. **Adoption Tracking** - Monitor which assets are being used, by whom, and how often
3. **Roadmap Inputs** - Gather data to inform capability investment decisions
4. **Playbook Distribution** - Publish and promote standardized approaches
5. **Pattern Recognition** - Identify emerging trends and successful practices to scale

## Interaction Pattern

- **Entry Point:** Analytics dashboard or portfolio view
- **Session Length:** 30-60 minutes (analysis sessions)
- **Primary Actions:** View dashboards, export reports, publish content, configure alerts
- **Preferred Output:** Charts, trends, comparative views, exportable reports

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Portfolio coverage | > 80% domains | Comprehensive visibility |
| Adoption trend visibility | Real-time | Timely decision making |
| Playbook adoption rate | > 50% | Standards being followed |
| Cross-program insights/month | > 5 | Active pattern recognition |
