The Goal of This Day

This is not a sales pitch and it is not a product demo. It is a conversation between two organizations that have a lot to build together — across DoW, DHS, HHS, CMS, and beyond. We leave knowing each other better, knowing each other's customers, and knowing exactly where we want to go next.

Agenda

9:00 AM Relationship
Welcome and Introductions

No slides. Each person introduces themselves and one thing they hope to get out of the day.

9:30 AM Relationship
Who We Are — eSimplicity

Our programs, our people, our pipeline. Not a capabilities brief — a conversation starter.

  • Current programs across DoW, DHS, HHS, CMS
  • Where Databricks already plays a role
  • What we want to build in the next 12–18 months
10:15 AM Relationship
Who You Are — Databricks

Products, professional services model, federal footprint. Not a pitch — context for everything that follows.

  • Product and ProServe model — how Databricks goes to market in federal
  • Federal agency footprint
  • How they partner with delivery firms
11:00 AM Relationship
Shared Stories — Customers We Have in Common

The richest part of the morning. Both sides share what they know about the agencies we both touch.

  • CMS / HHS OpDivs
  • USCIS — Databricks has significant presence here
  • Navy / Jupiter
  • DoW and DHS — stories we can tell together
12:00 PM Break
Lunch

Unstructured. Keep the conversation going.

1:00 PM Business
How Databricks Works With Delivery Partners

Databricks is product and professional services — they don't prime work. Understanding that shapes how eSimplicity positions alongside them.

  • ProServe delivery program — how do we get on it?
  • Dedicated SA time and pricing alignment
  • FedRAMP ATO white-label offerings for DoW
  • Building our certified Databricks bench
2:00 PM Business
Where We Want to Go — PoC and Pipeline

Concrete conversation about near-term opportunities.

  • 2–3 PoC opportunities to explore in the next 90 days
  • Active eSimplicity pursuits where Databricks expertise helps
  • Databricks customers who need delivery capacity
  • DoW strategy — eSimplicity has relationships Databricks lacks
3:00 PM Relationship
Open Floor — What Did We Miss?

Anything that didn't fit in structured sessions.

3:30 PM Relationship
Wrap-Up — What We're Taking Away

Each person names one takeaway and one follow-up they own.
