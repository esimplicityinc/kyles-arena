The Goal of This Day

This is not a sales pitch and it is not a product demo. It is a conversation between two organizations that have a lot to build together — across DoW, DHS, HHS, CMS, and beyond. We leave knowing each other better, knowing each other's customers, and knowing exactly where we want to go next.

Objectives

Morning Joint Session
Joint Session — All Groups

Foster shared understanding across all teams and establish a common baseline before breakout sessions.

  • Foster cross-agency alignment and shared understanding of Databricks capabilities across civilian, defense, and health teams.
  • Showcase past performance and use cases relevant to all three segments, highlighting how existing solutions could be adapted across agencies.
  • Establish a common baseline so breakout sessions can go deeper without re-covering fundamentals.
Lunch Networking
Networking & Informal Discussion

Unstructured time for cross-agency connection and relationship-building.

  • Encourage informal conversation between participants from civilian, defense, and health teams.
  • Begin informal identification of cross-agency collaboration opportunities without exposing sensitive pipeline data.
Afternoon Breakouts
Breakout Sessions — By Agency Group

Conduct targeted discussions for each group with relevant stakeholders and Databricks account managers.

  • Conduct targeted discussions for civilian, defense, and health groups with relevant stakeholders and Databricks account managers present.
  • Clarify Databricks access and integration options for each group, including contract vehicles and technical requirements.
  • Facilitate relationship-building and mutual value identification — build trust, share customer priorities, and surface specific collaboration opportunities.
