Quick Reference Guide

Portal Access Types

Login Required Partner portals require company credentials. Contact your PAM if you need access.

Public These resources are publicly accessible - great for initial research and sharing with team members.

Bookmark Your Portals

You'll access partner portals weekly (or daily during busy periods). Bookmark them now:

  • AWS Partner Central for deal registration
  • Each partner's training site for certification prep
  • Your PAM's contact info for quick questions

What Each Portal Offers

  • Deal Registration: Log opportunities to protect margins and track revenue
  • Training: Free courses, learning paths, and exam prep materials
  • Marketing: Co-branded assets, case study templates, logos
  • Support: Technical resources, architecture guidance, PAM contacts

Time-Saving Tips

  • Set up SSO where available to avoid login friction
  • Subscribe to partner newsletters for program updates
  • Check certification expiration dates quarterly
  • Register deals early - don't wait until close
